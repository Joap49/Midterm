Joann's Current Skincare Faves

by Joann Pilika

Hi, I’m Joann. I’m currently a student at NYU studying Integrated Design and Media. I’m a skincare junkie and beauty lover. I thought I’d share some things I’ve been loving. My skincare is somewhat combination right now. I feel dry, but I always look oily if I don’t have a full face of makeup on. I struggle with acne scarring after I got rid of my acne. My biggest contributor to clearing my face is the birth control pill and clindamycin/benzoyl peroxide mix. Those two worked wonders for my skin, but now I’m struggling with scarring.

Let’s start off with my morning routine. I start off my day with splashing my face with water, if I feel like my skins lacking hydration then I’ll cleanse with the Cetaphil cleanser. After that, I sometimes use Neutrogena Pore Refiner toner, sometimes I skip it altogether. It depends on how hydrated my skin feels. Right after this, I’ll go in with some of my holy grail products. The Ordinary’s Niacinamide serum, Marine Hyaluronic serum, and 10% Lactic Acid. The Niacinamide is one of my key ingredients to keep little breakouts at bay. After giving my skin all this love, I’ll finish off with the Embryolisse cream or Weleda’s Light Skin Food.

The only thing that is different at night is I’ll use The Ordinary’s Glycolic Acid Toner and I 100% will use Skin Food to moisturize. Some nights I’ll add the Aztec Healing Clay Mask or Murad’s Radiance Peel mask to help my scarring. I’m still looking for a hydration mask that I love, so if you have one, send it over! I hope this helps someone, but if you’re really struggling then I’d encourage you to visit your physician or dermatologist. My physician prescribed me all the acne medication that helped me! It never hurts to ask, right?
