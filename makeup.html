MAKEUP

Joann's Makeup Staples

by Joann Pilika

Makeup was how I got into beauty. I started experimenting with makeup when I was 12 years old and I couldn’t get enough of it. I grew obsessed with it and watching hundreds of makeup gurus on Youtube. I’ve switched gears though. I’m much more focused on skincare now and don’t invest as much time and money into makeup anymore. Don’t let that fool you, though! I still wear makeup every day and have some absolute STAPLES in my routine. Here’s what’s in my makeup bag.

I don’t step out of the house without mascara. I just can’t do it! My lashes are blonde and they grow downward. The worst possible combination, so when I don’t have my lashes lifted and tinted, I rely on Maybelline waterproof mascaras and a lash curler. Literally. Any of the Maybelline waterproof mascara will hold up those curled lashes ALL DAY. The lancome artliner completes my eyeliner and signature look. My eyebrow routine switches up pretty regularly. Sometimes I use Anastasia brow powder, sometimes Anastasia brow pencil in Taupe, but more recently I’ve been loving the bushy look that Boy Brow by Glossier has been giving me.

Face makeup? I try to avoid it, but the paler months need some coverage. I haven’t found my perfect concealer yet, but if you ever need a nice flush of color on your cheeks then there is only one option: Benetit by Benefit. It’s natural, it glowy, it’s perfect. A gamechanger. I’m still on the hunt for a stunning bronzer, but I’m not pressed about it after discovering Isle of Paradise self-tanner drops! I know I’m not a makeup artist, but I value looking dewy and natural.
